Scrambled Eggs

Description

This recipe was designed to be easy—a weekday lifesaver when you’re hungry and don’t have much time.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Capsicum
  • Ginger
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs and mix them in a bowl
  2. Pour the olive oil in the pan
  3. When hot, place the eggs and capsicum in the pan
  4. Add ginger after 5 minutes
  5. Add salt
  6. Remove from pan and serve hot
